EthicPrint · Manifesto

We don't always choose
the consequences
of our actions.
But we still create them.

This is why EthicPrint exists. Not to judge. Not to preach. To make visible what is usually invisible.

The problem

Every purchase is a vote

The companies we support every day use our money, our data, and our time to shape the world around us. They fund conflicts, damage ecosystems, exploit workers, and avoid the taxes that pay for public services.

Not always out of malice. Often simply because what they do is widely known but not always easy to verify. And because we, as consumers, rarely have an easy tool to see what we're really supporting.

What we do

Sources. Evidence. Transparency.

EthicPrint collects verified public sources — investigative journalism, NGO reports, regulatory decisions, academic research — and uses them to evaluate brands across four dimensions: conflicts & arms, environment, human rights, tax & transparency.

Every score is traceable. Every change is documented. Every source is open to scrutiny. There are no hidden sponsors, no paid placements, no conflicts of interest. No profit. Ever.

What we don't do

We don't judge people

We understand that not everyone can afford to make different choices every time. Economic constraints, availability, habit — these are real factors. EthicPrint doesn't pretend otherwise.

Our goal isn't to make you feel guilty. It's to give you the information you need to choose, when you can.

Why it matters

Awareness is where change begins

Individual choices alone won't solve systemic problems. We know that. But collective awareness — people understanding what they're actually funding — creates pressure that companies and governments cannot ignore. But to be honest, if even one person changes for the better, that alone fulfills our purpose.

EthicPrint is a small tool for a large shift: from passive consumption to informed choice. From convenience to consciousness.

Our principles
🔍
Radical transparency

Every score is documented with sources. Every change is public, dated and signed. Nothing happens behind closed doors.

🤝
Community driven

Anyone can propose corrections or new brands. But every change requires verified sources and passes human review before going live.

⚖️
Methodological honesty

We don't claim to have the final word. Scores reflect the best available evidence and are updated as new information emerges.

🚫
No profit. No ads.

EthicPrint will never be monetised. No ads, no paid placements, no sponsored scores. If it ever changes, fork the repo and start over.
